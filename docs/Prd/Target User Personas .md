## 5. Points Valuation Strategy (NEW)

### How Points Are Determined

WasteReturn points are tied to real market values:

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Commodity Market Price** | 60% | Current scrap metal/plastic market rates |
| **Processing Cost** | 20% | Cost to sort, clean, and process |
| **Environmental Offset** | 10% | Carbon credit value |
| **Demand Factor** | 5% | High-demand materials get bonus |
| **Social Impact** | 5% | Encouraging difficult-to-recycle items |

### Points-to-Cash Conversion

- **Standard Rate:** 2 points = ₦1 (subject to change based on points liability)
- **Minimum Cash-out:** 500 points (₦250)
- **Processing Fee:** 5% for bank transfers (waived for utility bill payments)

## 7. Sorting Quality Algorithm (COMPLETE)

Total Points = [(Base Points × Weight) × Sorting Multiplier × Cleanliness Factor × Loyalty Bonus] - Contamination Penalty

Where:

Sorting Multiplier = 1.0 (Mixed) to 1.5 (Perfectly Sorted)

Cleanliness Factor = 0.7 (Dirty/Contaminated) to 1.2 (Clean/Rinsed)

Loyalty Bonus = 1.0 to 1.25 (Based on 3-month history)

Contamination Penalty = 0-100% deduction based on severity



| Level | Description | Multiplier | Example |
|-------|-------------|------------|---------|
| **Gold** | Perfectly sorted, clean, prepared | 1.5x | PET bottles: labels removed, rinsed, crushed, caps separate |
| **Silver** | Well sorted, mostly clean | 1.3x | Bottles: labels on but rinsed, not crushed |
| **Bronze** | Sorted but needs work | 1.1x | Bottles with labels, some residue |
| **Mixed** | Combined recyclables | 1.0x | All recyclables in one bag |
| **Contaminated** | Wrong items mixed in | 0.5x | Food waste mixed with paper |
| **Hazardous** | Dangerous items in wrong bin | 0x + Warning + Possible Fee | Batteries in plastic bin |


### Contamination Penalty Matrix

| Severity | Description | Penalty | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|---------|
| **Mild** | Small amount of wrong items | 10% deduction | Few food scraps in paper |
| **Moderate** | Significant wrong items | 25% deduction | 20% food waste in recyclables |
| **Severe** | Mostly wrong items | 50% deduction | 50%+ contamination |
| **Hazardous** | Dangerous materials | 100% deduction + Warning | Batteries, chemicals, sharps |
